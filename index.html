About ICG

Innovative Construction for demanding projects

ICG is structured to handle complex, high-budget projects while staying close to every detail on site.

We are a specialist contracting company focused on luxury fit-outs, interior works and full construction. Our team blends design sensitivity with engineering know-how, making sure what is drawn on paper is actually buildable and durable.

With architecture, civil and mechanical engineering and a dedicated site architect under one roof, we coordinate everything from layouts and structure to MEP and finishes — so you deal with a single accountable partner.

  • One point of contact from concept to handover
  • Technical drawings, shop drawings & site supervision
  • Close coordination with suppliers and specialist subcontractors
  • Transparent planning, BOQs and progress updates
ICG core team

Architecture – concept design, layouts, interior design, details.

Civil engineering – structure, coordination, cost and quality control.

Mechanical engineering – HVAC, plumbing and building services integration.

Site architect – daily site presence, finishes, and detail execution.

This structure allows ICG to take on larger, high-budget projects while keeping the same level of attention that clients expect from boutique firms.
