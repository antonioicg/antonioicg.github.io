About ICG

A compact team for complex projects

ICG is structured to handle demanding, high-budget projects while staying close to every decision on site.

We specialise in luxury fit-outs, renovations and construction where details, coordination and finishes truly matter. Our work combines architectural thinking with robust engineering and day-to-day control on site.

From early layouts and technical drawings to final snagging, we keep design intent, budget and site realities aligned. You deal with one team that speaks both design and construction.

  • Architecture, structure and MEP considered together from day one.
  • Shop drawings, coordination and value engineering handled in-house.
  • Dedicated site architect for details, finishes and daily follow-up.
  • Transparent schedules, BOQs and progress reporting.
ICG core disciplines

Architecture · Concept, layouts, interiors and detailing.

Civil engineering · Structural coordination, phasing and cost control.

Mechanical engineering · HVAC, plumbing and building services integration.

Site architect · Presence on site, coordination of trades and finishes.

This multidisciplinary approach lets us take on larger, more complex projects while preserving the responsiveness of a boutique firm.

Approach

How ICG runs your project

A clear, engineering-driven process that reduces friction on site and keeps everyone aligned.

From first visit to final handover

Every project starts with a detailed understanding of your space, constraints and priorities. We then move through coordinated design, planning and execution with a single point of responsibility and regular communication.

Stage 01
Brief & Survey
Site visit, existing conditions, client objectives and budget alignment.
Stage 02
Design & Details
Layouts, technical drawings, BOQs and coordination between all trades.
Stage 03
Execution & Handover
Supervision, quality checks, testing, commissioning and closing snag lists.

Transparent communication

Flexible collaboration
